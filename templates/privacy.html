Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 7, 2026

1 Data We Collect

We collect the information you provide when you create an account (name, email, password) and the expense data you log while using Spendly, including amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions.

2 How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the Spendly service — to display your expenses, generate summaries, and personalise your experience. We do not use your financial data for advertising or sell it to any third party.

3 Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text. We take reasonable technical measures to protect your information from unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure.

4 Third-Party Services

Spendly may use third-party services for hosting and infrastructure. These providers are bound by confidentiality obligations and are not permitted to use your data for their own purposes. We do not integrate with any advertising or analytics platforms.

5 Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us. You may also request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time.
